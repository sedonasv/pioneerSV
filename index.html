About

Pioneer Stone Veneer offers beautiful, high quality stone veneer at wholesale prices. Please explore our website and check out some of our many residential and commercial installations.

Stones

We offer ten Stone Styles, with a wide variety of textures and shapes to fit your tastes, whether you are looking for something traditional or contemporary.

  • SPLIT LEDGESTONE has angular layers with many unique shapes.
  • CASTLESTONE has rectangular blocks with tightly fitting joints.
  • ASHLARSTONE has slightly worn, shallow texture blocks.
  • RUSTIC BRICK has old world style bricks with a weathered surface.
  • CHISELED LIMESTONE has roughly hand-carved blocks.
  • TUMBLED LEDGESTONE has long, weathered edge layers.
  • RIVERSTONE has naturally worn and rounded stones.
  • QUICK FIT has multi-stone, segmented layers with no grouting required.
  • FIELDSTONE has angled, highly variable stone.
  • STACKSTONE has tightly-fitting, wide layers of smooth rectangular stone.

Colors

Inspired by the striking natural landscapes of the Southwest, our nine Color Schemes range from subtle to vibrant.

  • BAJA has a blend of light tans and khaki.
  • WALNUT has intense shades of amber.
  • SEDONA has hues of red rock country.
  • GRAND CANYON has deep, earthy jewel tones.
  • IRONWOOD has rich grays with warm accenting.
  • SANTA FE has adobe earth tones of the high desert.
  • PRESCOTT has warm mountain shades.
  • MESQUITE has dark slate and charcoal.
  • ASPEN has a range of gold and silver hues.
